Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

Overview

USBCleaner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our macOS application.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No Tracking: We do not track your usage of the app.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use USBCleaner.

File Access

USBCleaner requires access to your external drives solely for the purpose of scanning and deleting specific files (such as .DS_Store and ._* files) as requested by you. The app processes these files locally on your device and does not transmit any file data to external servers.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Support page.
