Frequently Asked Questions

What files does USBCleaner delete?

USBCleaner targets hidden system files created by macOS, such as .DS_Store, ._* resource fork files, and .Trashes. It does not touch your personal documents, photos, or videos.

Is it safe to delete these files?

Yes. These files are metadata used by macOS (e.g., to store folder view settings). Deleting them is safe and often necessary when using the drive on non-Apple devices. macOS will simply recreate them if needed when you reconnect the drive.

The app says "Permission Denied". What do I do?

Ensure you have granted USBCleaner permission to access Removable Volumes in your Mac's System Settings > Privacy & Security > Files and Folders.
