Keep your USB drives clean and clutter-free with USBCleaner.

USBCleaner is a simple yet powerful utility designed to scan your external drives for hidden system files and other unwanted artifacts that macOS leaves behind. These files (like .DS_Store, ._*, and .Trashes) can be annoying when you use your drive on other devices like TVs, car stereos, or Windows PCs.

Key Features:
- **Smart Scanning**: Automatically detects connected USB drives and scans them for hidden clutter.
- **One-Click Cleaning**: Remove all identified junk files instantly with a single click.
- **Detailed Results**: See exactly what files are taking up space before you delete them.
- **Universal Support**: Runs natively on both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) and Intel-based Macs.
- **Safe & Secure**: Only targets specific system artifacts, keeping your personal data safe.

Perfect for photographers, DJs, and anyone who shares files between different operating systems. Download USBCleaner today and keep your drives spotless!
